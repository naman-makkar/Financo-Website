About Financo

Financo is the finance and consulting club of Bennett University, is a unique platform designed to provide students with an opportunity to enhance their knowledge and skills in finance and consulting. The club aims to develop financial literacy, provide networking opportunities, and promote professional development for all students of Bennett University.

Our Work

Fin-Talks

We organise talks by calling in various financial experts. These offer a dive into financial topics, introducing students of Bennett University to new terms and concepts of Finance and Economics.

Know more

Competitions

We conduct many finance related competitions which are fun and a great learning experience for beginners to get started in Finance. These competitions are mostly around equity markets, economics of World and India, and quizzes, etc.

Blogs

We release blogs throughout the year on various financial topics and news. Through these blogs, we aim to explain the concepts and terms of the financial world in the easiest way possible
